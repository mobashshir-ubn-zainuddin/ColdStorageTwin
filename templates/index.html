What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a virtual replica of a physical system that accurately mirrors its behavior and characteristics. In this application, we create a 3D numerical model of a cold storage facility to simulate temperature distribution and thermal dynamics.

The simulation solves the three-dimensional heat equation using the Finite Difference Method, allowing you to:

  • Model complex thermal behavior in cold storage chambers
  • Predict temperature distribution under different conditions
  • Optimize cooling system placement and parameters
  • Validate design decisions before physical deployment
